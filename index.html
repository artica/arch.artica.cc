At Artica – Creative Computing we had been fusing Arts & Technology to create meaningful human experiences.

We do physical computing at its full extension, bringing to our projects the set of skills and technologies that best feet the purpose of embedding digital technologies in everyday spaces, being it virtual, physical or hybrids.

In our labs we can construct new artifacts integrating hardware and software that best fit the architectural project, such as digital fabrication processes and machinery, electronics, robotics, mechanics, hydraulics, interactive projects, visual computing, artificial intelligence, analytics, augmented and virtual reality, mobile computing, IoT, just to name a few.
